Vocabtionary

Tenacious

(adj.) Persistent or determined

(Abraham's bold and tenacious demeanor as a student suggests that he is ready for great success in his career.)

Fervent

(adj.) Showing great enthusiasm or intensity

(Her fervent passion for music was evident in every note she played.)

Benevolent

(adj.) Kind and generous

(The benevolent millionaire donated a large sum of money to the local orphanage.)

Lexicon

(noun) A dictionary or vocabulary of a language

(She consulted the lexicon to find the definition of the unfamiliar word.)

Tyro

(noun) A beginner or novice

(As a tyro in the art of cooking, she followed the recipe meticulously.)

Amalgamation

(noun) The action, process, or result of combining or uniting

(The amalgamation of different cultures has enriched our society.)

Interpose

(v.) To insert or intervene between things or people.

(He interposed himeself between the arguing sibilings.)
